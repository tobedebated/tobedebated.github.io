We make YouTube videos on the most controversial issues of today to contribute for the culture of free, academically grounded and unbiased debate on the Internet.

Since its early years, Internet has been imagined as a free space for debate and sharing knowledge. By giving people access to information, it was believed to bring different worlds and perspectives together.
 

So, here we embark on a mission and create a project that is centered around the old idea of free and unbiased debate. So that we all can again become free thinkers and deliberately choose what to believe in.
 

But then something went wrong. We got researched, profiled and then served only the content we might like according to our previous behavior. Instead of active exploration, we got trapped in the past patterns.

 

 

 

 

As we’re a small team of friends, we can produce only one video in 2—3 months, from initial idea, all the way through research, script, design, animation and sound, to the final story you can all enjoy.

 

You can help us make it faster by supporting on Patreon. After all, there are so many important topics in the world that urgently need to be debated!

If you like what we’re doing and think that your project, cause or brand are based on a strong topic that should be debated — we’ll be happy to do it together.
